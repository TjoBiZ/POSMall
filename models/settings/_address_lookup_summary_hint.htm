How address help works in the storefront

Google Places and USPS help identify the customer's address, ZIP, ZIP+4, city and state. The local ZIP/tax coverage database is a separate required layer for matching that ZIP/state to the imported USA tax rate records used during checkout.

Address autocomplete alone does not load tax rates and does not enable tax calculation by itself. Tax calculation needs imported tax data connected to the product, service, category, subcategory or tax group. If Google, USPS or local coverage is unavailable, the form stays usable and the customer can enter the address manually.
